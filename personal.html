What did Anne grow?

From left to right: These tomato seedlings are making big progress over a month

This spring, I started growing tomato plants for the first time. The varieties I picked were Cherokee Purple, Mexico Midget, Ukrainian Purple, and Pineapple Tomato.

As a new tomato gardener, I was overwhelmed by the thousands of varieties of tomatoes that I can choose. This inspired me to come up with this project idea -- building a website that help people find the tomato varieties they want to grow based on their preference. The result is not perfect, but it's satisfying to see a small idea becoming a reality. Plus, looking at all the gorgeous tomato pictures just make me happy :) (Photo and data credits to the amazing National Gardening Association)

Thanks for stopping by. Hope this website helps new tomato gardeners to find the tomato varieties they like. If you ever think about starting growing tomatoes, this spring/summer would be a good time!
